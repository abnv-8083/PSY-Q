Psy-Q Refund, Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy  
At Psy-Q, we are committed to providing safe  and professional  online counsellin g services. 
This policy is designed to ensure fairness, transparency, and smooth scheduling for both 
clients and therapists.  By booking a session or purchasing a package, you agree to the terms 
below:  
Booking & Payment  
• All online counselling sessions must be booked in advance.  
• Payment must be completed to confirm your appointment.  
• Once booked, the time slot is reserved exclusively for you.  
Cancellation & Rescheduling  
If you need to cancel or reschedule your session, please notify us at least 24 hours before  the 
scheduled time.  
If cancellation/rescheduling is requested at least 24 hours in advance:  
You may choose either:  
• A full refund , or 
• A free reschedule  to the next available slot.  
If cancellation is made within 24 hours of the session:  
• The full session fee will be charged.  
• Refund/rescheduling will not be applicable, except under exceptional circumstances . 
No-Show Policy  
A no-show  occurs when a client does not attend the scheduled session without prior notice.  
In case of a no -show, the full session fee will be charged . 
No refund will be provided.  
Late Joining Policy  
• If you join late, the session will still end at the originally scheduled time.  
• Missed time cannot be extended or carried forward.  

• If you are late by more than 15 minutes , the session may be treated as a no-show  and 
marked completed.  
Prepaid Packages (Refund & Validity)  
If you purchase a prepaid package and wish to discontinue before completing all sessions:  
• Refund will be provided only for unused sessions . 
• Sessions already attended will be calculated at the regular individual session rate . 
• The remaining amount  will be refunded.  
Package Validity:  
• All packages must be used within the validity period mentioned at the time of 
purchase.  
• Sessions not used within the validity period may expire and become non-refundable . 
Workshops / Group Sessions  
Refunds for workshops or group sessions will follow the terms mentioned in the event 
registration details.  
In general:  
• Refund is possible only if cancellation is made within the specified timeframe before 
the event.  
• Once the event begins, refunds may not be available.  
Therapist Cancellation / Rescheduling  
If Psy-Q or the assigned therapist cancels or reschedules a session due to unavoidable 
circumstances:  
You will receive either:  
• A full refund , or 
• Priority rescheduling  at the earliest available time.  
Technical Issues (Internet / Platform Problems)  
Online counselling requires a stable internet connection and a suitable device.  
If the issue is from the client side  (poor internet, device failure, inability to join):  

• The session fee may not be refundable.  
• The therapist may continue the session if reconnection happens within the scheduled 
time.  
If the issue is from Psy-Q/therapist side  (platform failure, therapist connectivity issue):  
You will be offered either:  
• A free reschedule , or 
• A full refund , depending on the situation.  
Recording & Privacy Policy  
• Sessions are confidential and meant for therapeutic purposes only.  
• Recording (audio/video/screen recording) is strictly prohibited  without written 
consent from both the client and therapist.  
• Any unauthorized recording or misuse of session content may lead to immediate 
termination of services.  
Exceptional Circumstances  
We understand that emergencies can happen. If you believe your situation deserves special 
consideration (medical emergencies, unexpected travel, serious family issues), please contact 
our office and we will review the case individually.  Approval of refunds under exceptional 
circumstances will be at Psy-Q’s discretion.  
Safety & Emergency Disclaimer  
Psy-Q’s online counselling services are not meant for emergency situations . 
If you are experiencing:  
• suicidal thoughts,  
• self-harm risk,  
• violence or abuse,  
• severe mental health crisis,  
please contact your local emergency services immediately or visit the nearest hospital.  
Therapist Change Request  

If you feel uncomfortable or wish to change your therapist:  
• You may request a therapist change based on availability.  
• Psy-Q will support the request professionally and respectfully.  
Misconduct / Abuse Policy  
Psy-Q maintains a respectful and safe environment for clients and professionals.  
If a client engages in:  
• abusive language,  
• harassment,  
• inappropriate behaviour,  
• threats or misconduct,  
Psy-Q reserves the right to terminate the session and discontinue services.  
In such cases, refunds may not be provided.  
Non-Refundable Situations  
Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:  
• After the session has been completed  
• For late cancellations within 24 hours (unless approved under exceptional 
circumstances)  
• For no -shows  
• For dissatisfaction after service delivery (therapy outcomes vary and cannot be 
guaranteed)  
Refund Processing Timeline  
• Approved refunds will be processed within 7 working days . 
• Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method.  
• Payment gateway/bank processing times may vary.
