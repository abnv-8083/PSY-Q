Session Recording Policy  
Counselling sessions are private and confidential.  
Users are strictly prohibited from recording sessions (audio/video/screen recording) without 
prior written consent from the Professional and Psy-Q. 
Any unauthorized recording may lead to immediate termination of the session and account 
suspension/termination.  
Psy-Q does not record sessions unless explicitly stated and consent is obtained.
